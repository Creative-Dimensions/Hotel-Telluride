The Historic Sheridan Opera House

Pictured above, the historic Sheridan Opera House, located downtown between the Sheridan Hotel and San Miguel County Courthouse, has been Telluride's community center since 1913 hosting concerts, films, Telluride iconic festivals, and much more. Owned and operated by the Sheridan Arts Foundation, a 501(c)3 non-profit organization the Opera House hosts live theater, concerts and more throughout the winter. Taking in a show at the intimate Sheridan Opera House is a MUST for your Telluride visit. Past performers have included The Wailers, Jewel, Matisyahu, Three Dog Night, Chubby Checker, Dierks Bentley, Jackson Browne, Old Crow Medicine Show, Edward Sharpe and the Magnetic Zeros, John Prine, String Cheese Incident and many more. Visit SheridanOperaHouse.com to see their exciting lineup. The Opera House is also available for private wedding and party rentals!

To experience live music, theatre, comedy and the arts during your visit to Telluride, visit the Historic Sheridan Opera House schedule for Winter 2014.
