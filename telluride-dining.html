Dining

The Nook Restaurant


The Hotel Telluride offers exceptional dining experiences in our onsite restaurant, The Nook. Whether you would like to have a hearty breakfast to sustain your day on the slopes or enjoy a casual cozy meal paired with a fine glass of wine, The Hotel Telluride has something to suit your taste!

Breakfast Buffet

Daily: 7:00 am-10:00 am
Start your day off right at our hotel restuarant and give your stay at The Hotel Telluride a bed & breakfast feel. Our breakfast buffet includes: Daily fresh egg scramble, apple-smoked bacon, sausage links, home style potatoes, delicious pancakes, freshly baked pastries, muffins, asiago bagels, assorted cereals, fresh sliced fruits, yogurts and juices, and of course paired with a fresh cup of coffee. 

  • $13 per person (Hot Buffet) or $20 for two people when you book a breakfast package.
  • Ala Carte options are also available.


Fresh Baked Cookies Daily

Daily: 4:00 pm -5:00 pm

Every afternoon the sweet aroma of freshly baked cookies wafts from our Telluride hotel restaurant kitchen and fills the lobby, luring guests from their rooms. Our chocolate chip cookies are just the right sweet treat for a late afternoon snack. Plus, enjoy complimentary freshly brewed ice tea in the lobby all day long!

Après Ski & Dinner in The Nook

Daily: 4:30 pm -9:00 pm (winter only)

Beat the cold and satisfy your belly after an active day on the slopes at The Nook. Tapas, sharing plates, desserts and craft beers — everything you need for your Après Ski!



Our Après Ski unveils an array of winter comfort food for your choosing!

Unique among Telluride restaurants, The Nook offers casual American cuisine in a comfortable ski lodge setting. You will find all of your favorite appetizers with a twist, fresh dinner special made daily, a diverse children's menu, and homemade tavern fare using fresh, local ingredients.


 

A Word From Our Executive Chef, Simon Hadden

"My ‘made from scratch' philosophy paired with an emphasis on quality ingredients gives you a homemade meal every time you dine with us.  We strive to keep the menu diverse with daily specials featuring favorites of mine.  Whether you're preparing for a day on the slopes or unwinding with a nice glass of wine that soothes the palate, I hope to see you during your stay with us.  Thank you for choosing The Hotel Telluride."       -Chef Simon Hadden

nook ~ noun

1.  a corner of a room, or a part of a room cut off from the main part: a breakfast nook 
2.  a small recess or secluded spot; retreat
3.  a place affording peace and quiet
