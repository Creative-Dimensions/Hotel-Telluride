SKI RENTALS

The Hotel Telluride Ski Rental Information

Telluride Ski Resort is the perfect blend of pure mountain splendor, historic charm, world-class skiing, and unsurpassable beauty. Described as ‘Unmatched in North America', with virtually terrain for all ages and skier abilities, un-crowded lift lines, and 360 degrees of pristine beauty- we feel we are pretty special.

The Hotel Telluride is situated a short 3-blocks from the base of Lift 7 and a short 5-blocks from the base of Lift 8. With our complimentary shuttle and ski valet, we make getting to and from the slopes effortless!

To take a sneak peak at what Telluride Ski Resort has to offer, view Telluride's ski map.

Ski Rentals Options in Telluride

We realize the details of planning a ski vacation can tend to be overwhelming. To help narrow down the best rental options in Telluride, we have provided you three of our favorites.Below are the ski rental options that we rated the highest in quality, service, and with special Hotel Telluride discounts!

Black Tie Ski Rentals

Black Tie Ski Rentals is offering guests of The Hotel Telluride 20% off ski rentals for the 2011 and 2012 ski season. Black Tie provides the best equipment and outstanding service--all delivered at a time and place that's convenient for you. The best part... we come to you, no more rental shop lines! Experience Telluride's highest rated rental company- Black Tie Ski Rentals. To book your rental today and receive 20% off simply use the Discount Code: Corduroy to receive this offer when reserving online at either www.TellurideSkiRentals.com or telluride.blacktieskis.com.

Telluride Sports

With 8 rental locations in the town of Telluride and Mountain Village, no other rental shop offers Hotel Telluride guests more convenience and service than Telluride Sports. Hotel Telluride guests receive 20% off all ski and board rentals reserved at www.rentskis.com/hoteltelluride. We will also deliver your gear to the hotel for no extra charge! Simply choose delivery as your location option when renting. All gear is stored at our locations free of charge overnight, and equipment can be exchanged or returned at any store location. We promise it will be the easiest rental experience you've ever had!

Bootdoctors- Telluride

Bootdoctors and Paragon's award-winning ski and snowboard stores are ready to serve you in style! Recently cited by SKI magazine as "America's Best Bootfitters" we offer Telluride's largest selection of demo/rental skis and boards, top-of-the-line tunes and the region's widest choice of outdoor clothing and brands. We can deliver to you at your door or you can choose to visit one of our four Telluride and Mountain Village locations - two slope side Bootdoctors stores plus our Main street Paragon store and Mountain Village Ice Skate Rink and Rentals.
