TRAVEL INSURANCE

Why risk your vacation dreams when you can protect them with ease?

We strongly recommend travel protection, which gives you exceptional protection for events that can happen beyond your control. Our plan protects your travel investment for you and your traveling companions if you need to cancel your trip for covered reasons prior to departure, or if other covered unforeseen circumstances arise during your trip. You may review the VacationGuard® plan details here.

Two easy ways to protect yourself:

  1. The easiest way is through our Vacation Experts upon booking your hotel
  2. If you waited past your initial booking date, you can buy via our web page with your reservation number and trip cost, provided you have not paid your final trip deposit or already departed on your trip.

Description of Benefits

In just seconds, you can obtain a customized benefit package to protect both you and your traveling companions against events that can affect resort vacations. VacationGuard® provides innovative benefits that give you true vacation peace of mind, like:

  • Trip Cancellation & Interruption
  • Protections for Job Loss and other perils
  • Trip Delay benefits
  • Emergency Medical Evacuation & Repatriation
  • Medical Expense for Injury & Sickness
  • Accidental Damage to your Rental unit
  • Lost, Stolen, or Delayed Baggage
  • Travel Assistance Protection, Worldwide
  • Protection against Weather
  • Many other benefits too!

Valuable enhancements when booking through us:

1. Free look

You have a 21 day free look with VacationGuard® plans, giving you peace of mind to review your benefits and appreciate their value. If for some reason, you wish to cancel within that time, and you have not traveled or filed a claim, just contact your selling agent and you will receive a 100% refund.

2. Pre-existing Medical Condition Exclusion can be waived

The Pre-Existing Medical Conditions exclusion is waived if you enroll in this Protection Plan at or before your Final deposit with us, and if you purchase this Plan for the full cost of your trip.

3. Trip Inconvenience Benefit

Weather is unpredictable but protecting your fun doesn't have to be. Subject to plan terms, we protect your ski vacation if weather shuts the mountain down, as well as golf rounds if weather suspends or cancels your play.

4. Coverage on your protection plan beginning and end

We understand your vacation begins when you leave your home. As such, your VacationGuard® plan automatically extends your trip protection benefits, up to 2 days before your check-in date, and follows you back home, up to 2 days after your check-out date.

Have the right protection for your trip with VacationGuard® Call your reservation agent to get your travel peace of mind.
