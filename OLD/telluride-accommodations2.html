ACCOMMODATIONS

During your stay at The Hotel Telluride, you will love our guestrooms and hotel suites with breath-taking mountain views. Featuring all of the amenities you will need after a long day of skiing or hiking, each of our beautifully furnished, oversized lodging accommodations feature:

  • Complimentary wireless throughout the hotel
  • Individual climate control with heat and air conditioning
  • Spacious bathrooms with large tub and marble accents
  • AVEDA Bath products
  • Hair dryer
  • Private balcony or patio with mountain views
  • Windows that open for fresh mountain air
  • Relaxing seating group with overstuffed chairs and ottomans
  • Iron and ironing board
  • Refrigerator, microwave-toaster oven, flatware, plates and cups, coffee maker
  • 37-inch flat screen TVs with full cable and HBO
  • I-Pod Docking Stations
  • Humidifier
  • In-Room Safe
  • CD player/clock radio
  • Luxurious terry robes and slippers

The Hotel Telluride features two magnificent suites that are perfect for families and romantic getaways. The hotel suites can be configured into two and three bedroom arrangements to accommodate larger groups. Each hotel suite features special amenities in addition to our standard guestroom amenities:

  • Decorated living room
  • Gas fireplace
  • Flat screen television
  • Antique furnishings
  • Wet bar and original artwork
  • Large balconies and windows lend magnificent views
