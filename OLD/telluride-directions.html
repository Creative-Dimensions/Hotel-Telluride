Directions

From Telluride Regional Airport

Distance to hotel - 4.73 Miles to 199 N. Cornet Lane

  1. Start out going east on Airport Road/Airport Street/Last Dollar Road toward Basque Road.
  2. Continue to follow Airport Road/Last Dollar Road. 1.7 mi
  3. Turn left onto CO-145-SPUR. 2.7 mi
  4. Enter next roundabout and take the 2nd exit onto CO-145-SPUR/W Colorado Ave. 0.2 mi
  5. Turn left onto N Cornet Lane/N Cornet Sreet. 0.1 mi
  6. 199 N Cornet Lane is on the left.

From Montrose Airport

Distance to hotel - 69.1 Miles to 199 N. Cornet Lane

  1. Turn left from aiport onto N. Townsend Ave. (US-50).
  2. After one mile, N. Townsend becomes S.Townsend (US-550). Follow US-55- for 26.1 miles to Ridgway, CO.
  3. Turn right at light onto Main St. (SR-62) and follow 23.3 miles which turns into SR-145.
  4. Turn left onto SR-145 and follow 15.8 miles as it becomes Colorado Ave.
  5. Go straight on Colorado Ave. for 3.1 miles until Cornet St., the first street on the left in the Town of Telluride.
  6. Turn left on Cornet St.
  7. 199 N Cornet Lane is on the left.

Driving Directions from Denver, CO: Route A - Through Gunnison via US-285

  1. Take I-70 West towards Grand Junction.
  2. From I-70, take Exit 260 towards Colorado Springs onto state interstate 470.
  3. From 470, take US-285 South towards Fairplay.
  4. Take US-285 through Fairplay towards Poncha Springs
    5. .
  5. At Poncha Springs, take US-50 West over Monarch Pass towards Gunnison.
  6. Take US-50 through Gunnison towards Montrose.
  7. At Montrose, take a left onto US-550 South (Townsend Avenue).
  8. Continue on US-550 towards Ridgway.
  9. At Ridgway, turn right onto Highway 62.
  10. After approximately a little over 20 miles, turn left onto Highway 145 towards Telluride.
  11. In Telluride, you will go straight through the traffic circle, and pass the high school on your left. Then take the next possible left onto N. Cornet Lane. The Hotel Telluride will be on your left.

Driving Directions from Denver, CO: Route B - Through Grand Junction via I-70

  1. Take I-70 West towards Grand Junction.
  2. Take I-70 through Glendwood Springs.
  3. Do not go to Grand Junction - you will bypass the city by going through Palisade.
  4. From I-70, take a left onto US-50 West.
  5. Take US-50 through Delta.
  6. At Montrose, US-50 becomes US-550 South (Townsend Avenue).
  7. Continue on US-550 towards Ridgway.
  8. At Ridgway, turn right onto Highway 62.
  9. After approximately a little over 20 miles, turn left onto Highway 145 towards Telluride.
  10. In Telluride, you will go straight through the traffic circle, and pass the high school on your left. Then take the next possible left onto N. Cornet Lane. The Hotel Telluride will be on your left.
